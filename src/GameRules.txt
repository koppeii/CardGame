Players gain points by playing cards. There are 4 types of cards:
-- Point cards (add points to the player's total -- generally give more points values)
-- Attack cards (reduces points of another player -- generally give less points to the player)
-- Freeze cards (freeze another player for one turn -- generally give less points to the player)
-- Thief cards (steals a card from one player and gives it to the current player -- costs points to play)
